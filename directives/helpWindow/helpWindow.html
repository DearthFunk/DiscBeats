Disc Beats, is a drum machine in the shape of disc... Ta Da! ;)
Each ring in the disc is a sample and each slice of that disc is sample.
Click a beat to toggle it on/off.

HELP/INFO
Open this, the help window.

IMPORT/EXPORT
Import/Export your settings a text file (in case you clear your cache).

RANDOMIZE
Randomize the disc.

CLEAR
Clear the disc.

VOL
This is the overall volume of the application

SPD
This is how fast the step sequencer runs

LEN
This is how long the step sequencer is (from 4 to 32 beats)

KITS
There are 6 sample sets with preset FX for the TREMOLO, DELAY, and BIT CRUSHER.

KEYBOARD CONTROLS
Space Bar: used to start/stop playback
A: toggle Tremolo Active/Inactive
S: toggle Delay Active/Inactive
D: toggle Bit Crusher Active/Inactive
